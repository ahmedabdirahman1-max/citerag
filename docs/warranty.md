# Acme Supply Co. — Product Warranty

## Coverage
All Acme-branded products carry a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase. The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

## What is not covered
The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, unauthorized modification, or normal wear and tear. Consumable parts such as batteries and filters are excluded.

## Making a claim
To file a warranty claim, email support@acme.example with your order number and a description of the issue. Approved claims are repaired or replaced at no charge; we cover return shipping for valid defect claims.
