# Acme Supply Co. — Shipping Policy

## Shipping cost
Standard shipping is free on all orders over $50. Orders under $50 are charged a flat $5.95 shipping fee.

## Delivery times
Standard delivery arrives in 3 to 5 business days. Express delivery, available for an extra $14.95, arrives the next business day when ordered before 1 PM.

## Restrictions
We currently ship within the continental United States only. We cannot deliver to PO boxes or to addresses in Alaska, Hawaii, or U.S. territories.

## Order tracking
A tracking number is emailed once your order ships. Orders placed on weekends are processed the following business day.
