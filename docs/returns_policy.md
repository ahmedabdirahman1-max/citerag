# Acme Supply Co. — Returns & Refund Policy

## Return window
You may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.

## Restocking fee
Opened or non-defective returns are subject to a 15% restocking fee. This fee is waived if the item arrived damaged or if we shipped the wrong product.

## Clearance and final-sale items
Products marked "clearance" or "final sale" cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstances.

## Refund timing
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
